### Typography

**Primary Typeface: Lexend**
- Modern, clean, highly legible
- Weights: Thin, Light, Book, Medium, **Bold**, **Extrabold**
- Use for: Headings, UI elements, match cards, tournament names
- **Important**: Lexend is a free font and can be downloaded directly

**System Fallback: Proxima Nova**
- Use when Lexend is not available in software applications
- Weights: Regular, *Italic*, **Bold**, ***Bold Italic***
- Pre-installed on PC and MacOS systems

**Typography Hierarchy in Tournament System:**
- **Tournament Headers**: Lexend Bold or Extrabold
- **Round Labels**: Lexend Medium
- **Match Details**: Lexend Book or Regular
- **Body Text**: Lexend Light or Proxima Nova Regular
- **Scores**: Lexend Bold (large, prominent)
